## 1. Information We Access and Use

### A. SMS Messages and Notifications

With your explicit permission, the App accesses incoming SMS messages and notification content
**solely for the purpose of forwarding messages to a user-connected dumb phone**.

The App may access:

- Message content
- Sender phone number
- Group chat context (when applicable)

- Messages are processed locally on your device.
- Messages are **not transmitted to our servers**.
- Messages are **not used for analytics, advertising, or profiling**.

Forwarding occurs **only after you explicitly enable text syncing during setup**.

### B. Contacts

With your permission, the App accesses your contacts **only to allow you to share contacts with your
dumb phone and to display sender names instead of raw phone numbers**.

- Contact data is processed locally on your device.
- Contacts are **not uploaded to our servers**.
- Contacts are **not used for marketing, analytics, or advertising**.

### C. Phone Numbers

The App requires a smartphone number and a dumb phone number in order to enable text forwarding.

- These phone numbers are used **only to configure and enable text syncing**.
- We store:
  - The smartphone phone number
  - A flag indicating whether text syncing has been successfully set up
- We do **not** store message content or contact lists on our servers.

### D. Cached Group Chat Names

For the purpose of allowing users to mute group chats, the App may store **group chat names locally
on the device**.

- This data is cached on the device only.
- It is not uploaded or shared externally.
- It can be cleared by uninstalling the App or revoking permissions.

## 2. What We Do NOT Do

We do **not**:

- Sell personal data
- Share personal data with advertisers
- Use personal data for analytics or profiling
- Store SMS message content on our servers
- Access SMS or contacts without explicit user action

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **not** share personal data with third parties.

- No SMS or contact data is sent to third-party services.
- We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.
- We do not transmit message content to any backend service.

## 5. Data Retention

- SMS and contact data is processed **transiently in memory** and not retained.
- Cached group chat names are stored locally until cleared or the App is uninstalled.
- Phone numbers stored for setup remain until the user disables text syncing or deletes the App.
